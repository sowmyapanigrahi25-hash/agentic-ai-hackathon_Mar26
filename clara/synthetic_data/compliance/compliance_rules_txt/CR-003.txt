COMPLIANCE RULE: CR-003
Name: No Admission of Liability
Category: communication_standard
Severity: block
Applies To: letter

Description: Resolution letters must not contain language that admits fault, liability, or responsibility for customer losses.

Trigger Condition: Letter contains phrases like "our fault", "we are responsible", "our mistake", "we caused", or similar liability admissions

Remediation: Rewrite letter to acknowledge issue without admitting liability, use neutral language
