COMPLIANCE RULE: CR-001
Name: High Value Refund Approval Required
Category: refund_policy
Severity: block
Applies To: letter

Description: Any refund commitment exceeding $500 must receive manager approval before being communicated to the customer.

Trigger Condition: Resolution letter contains refund language with amount above $500 without manager approval flag

Remediation: Escalate to manager for approval before sending resolution letter to customer
