COMPLIANCE RULE: CR-010
Name: Refund Confirmation Format
Category: refund_policy
Severity: warn
Applies To: letter

Description: Refund commitments must include specific amount, timeline, and method in resolution letter.

Trigger Condition: Letter mentions refund but missing amount, processing timeline, or refund method details

Remediation: Add complete refund details including dollar amount, 5-7 business day timeline, and original payment method
