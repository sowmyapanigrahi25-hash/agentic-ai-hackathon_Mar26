COMPLIANCE RULE: CR-011
Name: Letter Sign-off Requirements
Category: communication_standard
Severity: warn
Applies To: letter

Description: All resolution letters must include proper closing with team name, company name, and case reference.

Trigger Condition: Letter missing closing signature line with team name, company name, or case reference number

Remediation: Add standard sign-off: "Sincerely, [Team Name] | [Company Name] | Case Ref: [complaint_id]"
