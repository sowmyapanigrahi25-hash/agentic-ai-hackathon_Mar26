COMPLIANCE RULE: CR-002
Name: SLA Breach Escalation Trigger
Category: escalation_trigger
Severity: block
Applies To: complaint

Description: Complaints that remain unresolved for more than 72 hours must be escalated to senior support team.

Trigger Condition: Time elapsed between complaint submission and current time exceeds 72 hours

Remediation: Immediately escalate complaint to senior support team with priority flag
